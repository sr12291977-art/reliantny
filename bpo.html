What We Do

ReliantNY operates as reliable sourcing network connecting companies with trusted Remote organised onshore and offshore Teams Solutions providers based on operational needs and business requirements.

How It Works

1. We analyze your operational or outsourcing requirements.

2. We identify suitable Remote organised Teams providers or SaaS vendors within our partner network.

3. We facilitate introductions between your business and selected providers.

4. If a partnership or service agreement is established, we may earn a referral commission from vendors.

Why Companies Use This Network

Businesses use this model to reduce time spent sourcing vendors, improve service alignment, and gain access to pre-vetted outsourcing and software providers across multiple industries.

Important Note

ReliantNY does not directly provide Remote organised Teams staffing or SaaS software. We act as a neutral connector facilitating introductions between businesses and service providers.
