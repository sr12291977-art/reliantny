Custom Technology Vendor Partnerships

We connect companies with industry-specific SaaS vendors through curated partnerships, helping organizations discover the right tools to improve efficiency, automation, and growth.

What We Do

ReliantNY operates as a B2B Technology partner network, connecting businesses with carefully selected software vendors that solve specific operational and industry challenges.

Instead of traditional consulting or generic software recommendations, we focus on structured introductions between companies and Custom Technology Solutions providers that align with their exact business needs.

How It Works

1. We understand your business requirements and current workflows.

2. We match you with relevant SaaS vendors that specialize in your industry.

3. We facilitate introductions and partnership discussions where appropriate.

4. When a successful partnership or subscription is formed, we may earn a referral or partner commission from vendors.

Why Businesses Use This Model

This approach saves time spent researching software, reduces mismatched vendor selection, and gives companies access to solutions already validated within their industry ecosystem.
